### When we think about
coziness and safety in games, we often think about it being styled as "wholesome" games. You know the kind, where you run a tea shop, or baby sit cats, or manage your uncle's old farm. These are games styled to a particular *flavour* of coziness.

But something I've realized about myself is that there's a specific feeling of 'coziness' I really enjoy, and that's watching the world end from a safe place.
